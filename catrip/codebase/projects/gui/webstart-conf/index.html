caTRIP: Cancer Translational Research Informatics Platform

The Cancer Translational Research Informatics Platform (caTRIP) aims to solve the difficult translational research problem of outcomes analysis. When a patient enters the clinic, the oncologist should be able to look across a cohort of patients with similar characteristics to help inform treatment. We solve this problem by leveraging caGrid to perform distributed queries across caTissue CORE, CAE, the Duke Tumor Registry, and the caIntegrator SNP database. The user interface is metadata driven, providing a flexible way to build queries and mine results.

*Note: in order to switch to a different instance (e.g. run the production and then afterwards the test instance) you will have to delete the ".caTRIP" folder in your home directory (e.g. C:\Documents and Settings\MCCON012\.caTRIP where MCCON012 is your login name).
